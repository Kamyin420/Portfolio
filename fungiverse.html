fungiverse

Fungiverse is a website that explores the use of mycelium within a new design context.

The website highlights how fungi, and mycelium in particular, are being used in unexpected ways. As a growing biomaterial, mycelium is increasingly explored by designers as a sustainable alternative to traditional materials.

With this project, I am aiming to showcase these emerging techniques and spark curiosity about the future potential of mycelium in design.
