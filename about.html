About

In my design journey, I am not bound by conventions. This allows me to focus on ideas without thinking if they are right or wrong. One idea progresses into the next, creating a unique web of ideas, like pieces of a puzzle that were never meant to fit in the first place.

I am a social person, I really enjoy getting to know people, to interact with them and have a laugh with them. People often describe me as open and I try to treat everyone as I would like to be treated myself. For me, life is all about connecting with each other.

I enjoy trying new things and failing at them. Every week is different and I love the challenge it brings me.

In my free time I rarely sit still, I work out a lot, go to museums and watch a lot of movies. I’m always looking to push myself, both physically and mentally.

Feel free to reach out for a cup of coffee, I’m always open to a good conversation.
